About Davido

Davido (David Adedeji Adeleke) is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He was born on November 21, 1992, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA, but relocated to Lagos, Nigeria, at a young age.
Davido began his music career in 2011, releasing his debut single "Back When" in 2012. However, it was his 2012 single "Dami Duro" that brought him widespread recognition in Nigeria. He has since released several successful albums and singles.
Davido has won numerous awards, including;
  • BET Award for Best International Flow (2019)
  • MTV Africa Music Award for Best Male Artist (2018)
  • African Music Award for Artist of the Year (2018)
  • Headies Award for Artiste of the Year (2018)
